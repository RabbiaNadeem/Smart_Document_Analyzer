Digital Marketing Campaign Proposal — Q2 2026

This proposal outlines a digital marketing campaign for Q2 2026 targeting millennial consumers through social media platforms. The campaign budget is $150,000 with expected ROI of 3.5x within 6 months. Key strategies include influencer partnerships, targeted Facebook ads, and Instagram Stories campaigns. Success metrics focus on engagement rates, conversion tracking, and brand awareness growth.

Target audience: millennials aged 25–34. Primary platforms: Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok. Timeline: Q2 2026 (April 1, 2026 through June 30, 2026). Projected revenue at target ROI is approximately $525,000.

Partners mentioned for planning purposes include Facebook Inc., Instagram LLC, and TikTok for paid placements.
